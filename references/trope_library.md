# Trope Library — Power Fantasy Patterns

Each trope defines the core pattern, standard scene count, and a reusable scene template. Pick one as the backbone; mix in elements from others for variety.

---

## 1. 扮猪吃虎 (Pig-Eating Tiger — Hidden Identity)

**Core**: Protagonist hides overwhelming power/status; antagonists treat them as worthless; truth revealed at climax.

**Scene count**: 5-7

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Arrival — in plain clothes, dismissed by gatekeeper/front desk | Subtle hint of power (a glance, a card) | |
| 2 | Underestimation — mocked by local elites at a public venue | One retort that silences the room | |
| 3 | Escalation — antagonist calls in heavyweight backing | Protagonist unimpressed | Backer arrives and kneels |
| 4 | Revelation — identity revealed through artifact/name/title card | Crowd reaction shots | Antagonist collapses in terror |
| 5 | Aftermath — protagonist walks away casually | Onlookers' shocked commentary | Former mockers beg for forgiveness |

## 2. 废柴逆袭 (Waste-to-Warrior)

**Core**: Disgraced or bullied protagonist gains sudden power (system, inheritance, awakening); systematically overturns every insult.

**Scene count**: 6-8

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Rock bottom — mocked, expelled, or publicly humiliated | A quiet vow | |
| 2 | Awakening — power system activates, hidden mentor appears | First success at a trivial challenge | |
| 3 | Training arc — rapid power growth shown through montage | Exceeding a known benchmark | |
| 4 | First return — former bullies test the protagonist | Casual one-shot defeat | Bullies' disbelief |
| 5 | Tournament/Exam — public showcase of new abilities | Dominating each round | Winning against the odds |
| 6 | Confrontation — bully leader or rival makes final stand | Protagonist reveals true level | Complete and total defeat |

## 3. 重生复仇 (Rebirth Revenge)

**Core**: Protagonist dies betrayed; returns to a past point in time with full knowledge; systematically destroys former enemies while protecting allies.

**Scene count**: 5-8

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Death & Rebirth — the betrayal and the return | Recognition of the past-life moment | |
| 2 | First move — exploit a future event to gain advantage | Enemy's confusion at failed scheme | |
| 3 | Re-encounter — meet a past-life enemy acting confident | Protagonist's cold, knowing smile | Enemy feels inexplicable dread |
| 4 | Layered scheme — protagonist dismantles a trap that killed them before | Each layer crushed publicly | Enemy's shock |
| 5 | Climax — final confrontation with the betrayer | Protagonist reveals knowledge of the betrayal | Betrayer's complete collapse |

## 4. 战神归来 (God of War Returns)

**Core**: Retired supreme warrior returns to civilian life; everyone takes him for a nobody; old enemies and new punks learn the hard way.

**Scene count**: 5-7

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Homecoming — returns to hometown/ordinary job/estranged family | Cold reception, silence in response | |
| 2 | Civilian insult — local thug/hostile coworker pushes too far | One effortless counter | Thug flies across the room |
| 3 | Escalation — thug brings gang/backer: an underworld boss | Boss recognizes the protagonist | Boss kneels, calls out the title |
| 4 | Domain-level threat — an old enemy's forces appear | Protagonist stands alone against army | Enters combat state |
| 5 | Resolution — enemy defeated; civilian bystanders realize who they misjudged | Protagonist returns to quiet life | Ignored until next time |

## 5. 神医降世 (Divine Doctor)

**Core**: Master healer arrives in a modern setting; no one believes in traditional medicine; protagonist saves terminal patients and humiliates arrogant Western doctors.

**Scene count**: 5-6

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | First impression — dismissed by modern hospital staff | Saves a minor emergency in the lobby | |
| 2 | Dismissal — senior doctors mock traditional methods | Treats a patient doctors gave up on | Patient walks out |
| 3 | VIP emergency — an untouchable figure falls critically ill | Protagonist named as last resort | Miraculous recovery |
| 4 | Hospital confrontation — antagonistic doctor demands proof | Public demonstration | Spectator doctors' shock |
| 5 | Recognition — medical establishment forced to acknowledge | Protagonist walks away, unbothered | |

## 6. 失忆归来 (Amnesiac Return)

**Core**: Protagonist had memory sealed but was once an ultimate power. Amnesia breaks; everyone who mocked them during the weak period faces the truth.

**Scene count**: 6-8

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Weak state — protagonist in low-status civilian life | Bullied, says nothing | |
| 2 | Trigger — a specific person/place/artifact begins awakening memories | Strange familiarity that unnerves an enemy | |
| 3 | Partial awakening — first sign of power leaks out | Defeat a small challenger casually | |
| 4 | Memory flood — full memories return | Cold fury at those who exploited the weakness | |
| 5 | Reckoning — confront those who betrayed during amnesia | Each confrontation a one-sided beatdown | |
| 6 | Return — allies from the past appear | World acknowledges the return | |

## 7. 身份互换 / 冒充 (Identity Swap / Impersonation)

**Core**: Protagonist is forced to impersonate a powerful figure, or a nobody steals the protagonist's identity. Truth comes out at critical moment.

**Scene count**: 5-7

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | The swap — setup of the identity confusion | Awkward moments that should expose but don't | |
| 2 | Early challenges — people test the faked identity | Protagonist passes tests with surprising skill | |
| 3 | Suspicion — one sharp observer begins to doubt | Protagonist outmaneuvers the doubter | |
| 4 | The impostor revealed — the fake identity is publicly challenged | Protagonist's real identity emerges | Real identity is even more terrifying |
| 5 | Fallout — those who mocked the fake now face the real | |

## 8. 金手指系统 (System / Cheat Power)

**Core**: Protagonist receives a game-like system interface that grants quests, skills, items; levels up rapidly; anyone who looks down on them becomes a stepping stone.

**Scene count**: 5-7

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | System awakening — desperate moment triggers system | First quest completed casually | |
| 2 | Low-level grind — rapid advancement through system quests | People notice the sudden improvement | |
| 3 | Hidden shop / skill unlock — system grants a rare ability | New ability overwhelms a mid-tier opponent | |
| 4 | Event / tournament — system-mode activated | Dominating performance | System reveals hidden title |
| 5 | Boss fight — antagonist wields their own cheat, but weaker | Protagonist's system counters perfectly | |

## 9. 赘婿觉醒 (Live-in Son-in-Law Awakens)

**Core**: Protagonist is the mocked husband living with the wife's family; secretly possesses immense power or heritage; revelation upends the entire family dynamic.

**Scene count**: 5-7

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Daily humiliation — mocked at family dinner, wife embarrassed | Protagonist's calm patience | |
| 2 | Small intervention — a family problem that only protagonist can solve | Quietly resolves a crisis | |
| 3 | Outsider threat — powerful enemy targets the wife's family | Protagonist steps forward | |
| 4 | Identity reveal — the enemy recognizes a token/title/badge | Enemy retreats in terror | |
| 5 | Family reckoning — the family learns the truth | Mother-in-law's horror | Wife's conflicted feelings |

## 10. 退婚逆袭 (Broken Engagement Revenge)

**Core**: Protagonist's engagement is cancelled because they're deemed worthless; years later returns as a supreme power; ex-fiance's regret is the main payoff.

**Scene count**: 5-7

**Template**:
| Scene | Purpose | Minor Payoff | Major Payoff |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | The break — engagement publicly cancelled, humiliated | Protagonist's dignified silence | |
| 2 | Years later — news arrives: the protagonist has achieved something massive | Ex-fiance's family dismisses the rumor | |
| 3 | Return — protagonist reappears at a major event | Everyone treats them as a nobody until... | |
| 4 | Encounter — face the ex-fiance and the new partner | Protagonist's complete indifference | Ex-fiance realizes what they lost |
| 5 | Showdown — new partner challenges protagonist | Overwhelming defeat | New partner's shame and ex-fiance's regret |

## Mixing Tropes

Combine two tropes for freshness:
- 战神归来 + 扮猪吃虎: returns as a civillian, everyone treats him as a nobody, but he IS the war god
- 废柴逆袭 + 金手指系统: the cheat system IS the awakening
- 重生复仇 + 神医降世: reincarnated divine doctor in a modern hospital

Once you pick a trope and outline scene structure, generate each scene following the output format in `references/output_format.md`.
